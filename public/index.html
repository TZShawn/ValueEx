We’ll help you get Connected
with Interested Buyers.

Listing your Item is simple and easy. Sign up to create your account, where you can easily list and manage your items. We’ll put it up on our catalogue and notify you when we’ve found a potential buyer. From there, you’ll discuss where to finish the transaction, saving yet another item from joining a landfill.

Save
Money.

Buying new items probably makes you feel great, but not for long. Brand new often costs far more than any secondhand alternative, even though barely used items last just as long for well below the price.

Whether you're a buyer or seller, ValuEx will help you make money on old items and save money on used ones that will meet your needs!

Help the
Planet.

Billions of tons of waste are produced each year around the world. Of this, a significant number are perfectly usable items that could have been reused or repurposed.

We want to help the planet by connecting people like you, people who see the value in buying secondhand and want to help keep Earth cleaner and greener.

Join the
Community.

With our growing network of buyers and sellers, you're sure to find something that catches your eye!

Encourage others to consider if something can be repurposed or resold before it heads to the landfill. With your contributions to our community, we'll move forward to a greener future together.

Give Waste, Get Value

You may have items lying around that you no longer use, and chances are you’re ready to throw it away. But it still has value. We’ll help connect you with others who’ll be willing to buy your items, ready to give it new life and purpose.

Everyone can do their part, and we’re doing ours by diverting waste away from landfills.
